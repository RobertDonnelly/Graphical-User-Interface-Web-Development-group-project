About Us



Who Are We?

Comp Tek - the Computer and laptop Specialist is a leader in Ireland for Gaming Pcs, Desktops, Laptops and accessories.

Comp Tek offer all the latest Pcs and laptops from all the leading brands. While a 3 year warranty on new products ensures peace of mind.

Comp Tek is now scaling up its online operation via the new webstore you're on currently, which is fast, optimised for mobile devices & offers multiple currencies for international customers. We're effectively taking the renowned Comp Tek retail store experience & bringing it online to serve a bigger market.



How Did We Start?

We started as an online shop in April 2016, as we felt the most efficient way to deliver the best deals in Ireland was through the web.

Due to the boom in popularity of the website, as well as constant demand and feedback from our customers, we opened our first retail store in Corbett Court Shopping Centre in Galway in August 2017.



Where Can You Find Us?

We currently have 1 store in Ireland, with our head office and store in Corbett Court Shopping Centre, Galway.

Due to phenomenal widespread success and demand, we are ever expanding, and are in the process of opening more stores nationwide, creating many more Irish jobs in the process, and bringing the lowest prices and fantastic customer service closer to you.



Customer Feedback

Our flagship retail store in Corbett Court, Galway City has cultivated a reputation for excellent prices & customer service, receiving a phenomenal amount of 5 star reviews across 100s of user-generated social reviews. The domain expertise of staff members in particular has repeatedly been cited, while the rapid turnaround time for submitted repairs (from 1 hour) is unprecedented.



Staff Force

Comp TeK is an Irish company opened in 2009 with a workforce of 3. Over the years it has increased at a steady rate and now currently provides over 30 jobs locally between our store and head office.
